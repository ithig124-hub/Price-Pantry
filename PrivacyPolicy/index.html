Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PricePantry! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our grocery price comparison application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Our Commitment

We believe in transparency and giving you control over your data. PricePantry is designed with privacy in mind.

2. Information We Collect

PricePantry collects minimal information to provide you with the best grocery price comparison experience:

  • Search Queries: Products you search for to compare prices across stores
  • Favorites: Products you save to your favorites list (stored locally on your device)
  • Shopping Lists: Lists you create to track groceries (stored locally or synced if you opt-in)
  • Price Alerts: Email address (optional) if you choose to receive price drop notifications
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide accurate price comparisons across Australian grocery stores
  • Send price alert notifications (only if you opt-in)
  • Improve our app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services (anonymized data only)

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • Local Storage: Favorites and shopping lists are primarily stored on your device
  • Encryption: All data transmitted to our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
  • No Sale of Data: We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • Minimal Collection: We only collect data necessary for app functionality

5. Third-Party Services

PricePantry may use the following third-party services:

  • Price Data Providers: To fetch current grocery prices from Australian stores
  • Email Services: To send price alert notifications (Resend)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics to improve app performance

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Delete: Clear your favorites, shopping lists, and alerts at any time
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from email notifications anytime
  • Export: Download your shopping lists and favorites

7. Children's Privacy

PricePantry is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Australian Privacy Law

PricePantry complies with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). Australian residents have specific rights regarding their personal information under this legislation.
